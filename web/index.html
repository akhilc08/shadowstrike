Special Moves (cost energy):
Down + Special = Projectile (30 energy)
   Inferno: Fireball · Voltaic: Bolt Shot · Umbra: Shadow Surge (slower, 70 dmg, slows opponent)
Forward + Special = Dash (25-30 energy)
   Inferno: Flame Rush · Voltaic: Thunder Dash · Umbra: Void Dash (teleport behind, 75 dmg)
Special alone = Uppercut (free, no energy cost)
Combos: Chain light attacks (Z/J x3), cancel into heavy or specials.
Blocking: Hold block to reduce damage to 20% chip. Blockstun is shorter than hitstun.
Energy: Gain 8 energy per hit landed. Specials cost 30.
Win condition: Best of 3 rounds, 60 seconds each. KO or highest health at time-up wins.
P1: WASD move · Z light · X heavy · C special · V block
P2: Arrows move · J light · K heavy · L special · ; block
Special moves: ↓+Special = Projectile · →+Special = Dash/Teleport · Special alone = Uppercut (costs energy, Uppercut free) · F1 = Debug WASD move · Z light · X heavy · C special · V block
